        • Delivery of cargo or shipments depends on several factors, including the Estimated Time of Departure (ETD) and the Estimated Time of Arrival (ETA). Typically, sea cargo can take anywhere from 45 days to 60 days and may delivered earlier depending on the distance between the origin and destination ports, countries, weather conditions, and any potential customs delays.

          For a more accurate delivery time, please use the tracking number or unique combination of numbers and possibly letters or ID provided by Homeway Express to receive an update, or contact our customer support team for detailed information regarding your specific shipment or cargo.
